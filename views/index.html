Matthew Fong
Game Designer & Developer

I graduated Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), NY, with a Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in game design and development, and a completed immersion (half minor) in music. Rather than game design and development, as my program covered a wide range of concepts, a better way of describing my skills and practices would be designing and programming rich interactive experiences. These experiences are showcased through my projects, ranging from polished games to high-scale interactive web applications.

I have programmed in various software languages such as C++, JavaScript, C#, Swift; and am familiar with tools such as HTML5, CSS3, ES6, NodeJS, ReactJS, ExpressJS, REST, MVC framework, JQuery, AJAX, JSON, Object-Oriented Programming, MongoDB, Redis, Websockets (socket.io), XML, and Canvas.
I also have worked with various software programs, including Visual Studio, VS Code, Brackets, Autodesk Maya, Unity, Adobe Photoshop, GIMP, Processing, MySQL and Xcode.

As a developer, I am eager to continue growing, learning new concepts and challenging myself in both software development and web development.
