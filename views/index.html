Matthew Fong
Game Designer & Developer

I graduated Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), NY, with a Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in game design and development. I am currently pursuing my Master's of Science (MS) degree in game design and development also at RIT, expected May 2027. Rather than game design and development, as my program covers a wide range of concepts, a better way of describing my skills and practices would be designing and programming rich interactive experiences.

My main area of focus is gameplay programming, however, 3D art and modeling is always a field that intrigues me. As a developer, I am eager to continue growing, learning new concepts and challenging myself in both aspects of game design and game development.

I have programmed in software languages such as C++, JavaScript, C#; and am familiar with tools such as HTML5, CSS3, Sass, ES6, NodeJS, ReactJS, ExpressJS, REST, MVC framework, JQuery, Object-Oriented Programming, Websockets (socket.io), and Canvas.
I also have worked with various software programs, including Unreal Engine 5, Blender, Visual Studio, VS Code, Unity and Autodesk Maya.
