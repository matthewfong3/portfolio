Adventure Time

Overview

Adventure Time is a simple 2D side scrolling retro platform game. The player must traverse through the level while fighting various different types of enemies and avoiding spike traps. Throughout the level, the player may find and collect gems and health (hearts) to replenish their health after surviving previous encounters with enemies.

Tools used

Unreal Engine 5, Paper2D, Blueprints

Role

- Programmed player controls, such as movement, jumping, attacking
- Programmed various enemy AI logic using AI Controller, such as edge detection, enemy detection, simple autonomous behavior (seeking when the player is in range or free roaming)
- Implemented player health system, syncing update logic with widget blueprints to display current health or game over (death) screen

Role (Cont.)

- Developed the game states and transition logic between the different game states
- Designed the layout and flow of the level

Platform

Windows

Duration

Less than 4 weeks

Team size

1
